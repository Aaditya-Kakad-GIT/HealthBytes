About Health Bytes

üéØ Why This App Was Made

Health Bytes was created to address the growing need for easy access to nutritional information. In today's fast-paced world, people often struggle to make informed dietary choices due to lack of readily available nutrition data. We wanted to bridge this gap by providing a simple, user-friendly platform that makes nutrition tracking accessible to everyone.

Our mission is to empower individuals to take control of their health through better understanding of what they eat, ultimately leading to healthier lifestyle choices and improved well-being.
